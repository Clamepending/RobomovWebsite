Soldering

Soldering is used to make an electrical connection between two parts. It involves melting the solder with the soldering iron (at around 400 degrees C) and touching the molten solder onto where the connection is needed. (image of soldering) There are excellent soldering tutorials on youtube such as https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VxMV6wGS3NY Hint: To keep the female t connector from deforming, it is a good idea to plug the male one in to keep the shape while soldering. (image)

Solder the black wire to the horizontal pin and red wire to the vertica pin of the T connector. (image)
